Google Search Console Setup Instructions

Step 1: Add Your Site to Google Search Console

  1. Go to Google Search Console
  2. Click "Add Property"
  3. Choose "URL prefix" and enter your domain
  4. Choose HTML file verification method
  5. Download the verification file and upload it to your /public folder
  6. Click "Verify"

Step 2: Submit Your Sitemap

  1. In Google Search Console, go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
  2. Add your sitemap URL: https://your-domain.com/sitemap.xml
  3. Click "Submit"

Step 3: Set Up Enhanced Ecommerce (for affiliate tracking)

  1. Link Google Analytics to Search Console
  2. Enable Enhanced Ecommerce in GA4
  3. Set up conversion tracking for affiliate clicks

Important URLs to Submit:

  • Main sitemap: /sitemap.xml
  • News sitemap: /news-sitemap.xml (if created)
  • Image sitemap: /images-sitemap.xml (if created)

Note: After verification, it may take 24-48 hours for Google to start indexing your pages.
